About My Co-op Placement

I completed my Co-op with RGB Eco-Studies, a hands-on environmental education program focused on outdoor learning, leadership, and sustainability.

My role involved visiting schools and presenting to Grade 10 students, explaining what Eco-Studies is, why it matters, and how it differs from traditional classroom learning.

I also designed and developed a website for the program, applying technical and creative skills in a real professional environment.
